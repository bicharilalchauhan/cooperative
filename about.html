Who We Are

Your Future, Owned by You.

Community Credit Union is a member-owned financial institution. Unlike traditional banks, we operate as a not-for-profit cooperative where every member is an owner with a voice. Our earnings are returned to you in the form of better rates and lower fees.

Our Mission

"To empower the next generation with the financial tools and knowledge needed to achieve true independence."

We believe financial literacy is a right, not a privilege. By focusing on youth, we build a foundation for a healthier economic future for our entire community.

Our Story

The cooperative movement began as a simple idea: that working together, ordinary people could achieve extraordinary financial stability. Founded in 1982, Community Credit Union was born from a local initiative to provide fair lending to students and young entrepreneurs who were often overlooked by major banks.

From our humble beginnings in a community center basement to serving over 50,000 members today, our focus hasn't wavered. We remain committed to the democratic principles of the co-operative movement, ensuring every member has an equal seat at the table.
