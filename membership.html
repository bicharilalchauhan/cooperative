Who Can Join?

Membership is open to individuals who meet at least one of the following criteria:

  • check_circle Residents of the Metropolitan Area between the ages of 18 and 35.
  • check_circle Full-time students enrolled in accredited local universities.
  • check_circle Employees of our partner corporate organizations.
  • check_circle Direct family members of existing Community Credit Union members.
